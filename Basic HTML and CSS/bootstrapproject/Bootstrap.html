What is Valorant?

Valorant is a team based tactical first-person shooter developed by riot games

Accommodations:

With a variety of different agents you can play valorant with multiple play-styles.

Each Agent is fun to play in their own way with their abilities you can pull off some crazy stunts.

With a ton of different weapons you can play up close or far away.

Play what game mode suits you if you want to play ranked, casual, deathmatch or any other small game modes it has.
